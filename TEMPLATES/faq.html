WHY ARE LEAVES TURNING YELLOW?

Plant is under stress due to environmental changes or while coping with an acclimation period to its new home. Some older leaves might turn yellow and shed. Usually, it happens to the lower leaves.

WHY ARE LEAVES ROTTING?

Rotting leaves are a definite sign of over-watering and can also be the sign of root rot. Use a pencil or stick to fluff up the soil to allow oxygen get through and to help soil to dry out faster.
